The rise of Sundiata Keita

Sundiata Ke誰ta after living in exile at the age of 25, he amassed an army through alliances with nearby Mandinka people and faced Soumaoro in the battle of Kirina in 1235. Legend has it that Sundiata Keita defeated Soumaoro Kante in the battle because he was a more powerful magician than Soumaoro.

The Mali Empire

Sundiata was known as a big eater and constantly held feasts at his palace. His nickname is the "Lion King of Mali." He was the first king of the Mande people to use the title "Mansa", which meant "king of kings." He laid the foundation for a powerful and wealthy African empire and proclaimed the first charter of human rights, the Manden Charter.

The death of Sundiata

Sundiata Keita died in the Sankarani River afetr he tried to retreat from a battle. He drown in the river and his followers never saw him again. The legend says he was the character used for the cartoon of the Lion King
